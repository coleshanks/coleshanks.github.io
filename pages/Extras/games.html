Sony PlayStation

PLAYSTATION Final Fantasy VII 1997 Really cool. Amazing open world and good trajectory from Midgar's cramped corridors and kind of oppressive mechanical droning music. To then out into the world, first still feeling small because of how you could travel and then opening up progressively with each new vehicle. Ending with the airship that let u revisit old places with a full party and made the world feel complete. Materia system really fun once understood. Great story. Cloud's uncertain memory and past. Compelling and tragic backstories for basically all party members. Sephiroth such a cool villain. Looms over the whole game like a spectre. Amazing characters and developed world. Nobuo Uematsu composed one of the best soundtracks in any game ever
