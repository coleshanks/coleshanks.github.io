about me

Hi there! I'm a graduate of Electrical Engineering from UBC, currently living in Japan as part of the JET Programme. I'm passionate about learning Japanese, exploring history and culture, and reading—particularly sci-fi and fantasy.

In my free time, I enjoy programming—this website and some small games on my GitHub showcase my work.

Traveling is another one of my passions, and I've been fortunate to visit many countries during my time in Japan. Feel free to explore the rest of my website—there are some cool hidden gems if you look around!
